# HomeServices - Final (Website)

## Server Mount Order (VERY IMPORTANT)
- Webhooks BEFORE express.json():
  - /webhook/stripe
  - /webhook/stripe-billing
  - /webhook/verified-pro
- After that, use `app.use(express.json())` and mount admin/API routes.
